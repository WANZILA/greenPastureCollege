Green Pasture College Fees and Tuition Payments

Tuition and fees are established by the College Council and are subject to change without notice. Tuition and fees are charged per semester. A student’s admission letter will show all tuition and fee charges, payments and deferments associated with each semester. All College tuition and fees must be paid by the published deadline dates. Tuition and fees not paid or deferred by the payment deadline for each semester will result in late fees and may result in the cancellation of all classes.
