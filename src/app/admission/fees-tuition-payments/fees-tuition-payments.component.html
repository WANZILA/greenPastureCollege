Green Pasture College Fees and Tuition Payments

Tuition and fees are established by the College Council and are subject to change without notice. Tuition and fees are charged per semester. A student’s admission letter will show all tuition and fee charges, payments and deferments associated with each semester. All College tuition and fees must be paid by the set deadline.

The following non refundable fees are paid on subbmission of the application forms:
  1. Certificate: 30,000/=
  2. Diploma: 30,000/=
  3. Short Course: 30,000/=

Payment Procedures for Certificate and Diploma

  • The College fees payment policy allows students to make fees payments in five installments as shown below: Payment of all functional fees should be done on the 1st day of every semester
    • 40% fees by 3rd week of the semester
    • 30% fees by the 8th week of the semester
    • 100% fees by the 12 week of the semester
  • We don’t accept cash payment at the College. No College staff is allowed to receive fees from students. You can pay your all your fees through the following modes:

Direct deposit (over the counter) or Electronic Transfer to any of our Banks

Late Fees
The Late Registration Fees are charged to students who enroll following the close of the regular registration period for the semester. The Late Registration Fee is 30,000/= per semester
