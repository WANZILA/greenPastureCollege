Green Pasture College Admission Requirements

Admissions to Green Pasture College shall be open to all qualified national and international persons without discrimination. A student is admitted into an academic program when he/she meets the entry requirements into that program and level. Admissions into Green Pasture College is conducted under the guidance of the National Council for Higher Education and with the approval of the Senate

Click the tabs below to navigate through all the requirements.

  1. An applicant will be admitted upon fulfilling the following basic requirements
    • Academic achievement
    • Good character
    • Financial support
  2. The College reserves the final decision on matters of admission, retention, and withdrawal.
  3. A candidate admitted to a degree programme, diploma or certificate in any School shall comply with conditions and meet the guidelines set in the College’s admission policy. The admissions committee has the right to admit or refuse admission of any applicant.
  4. Registered students in another higher education institution in the Republic of Uganda or any recognized institution of learning who desire to be admitted to the College should apply for credit transfer through the academic registrar’s office

The minimum entry requirements for admission to an diploma programme are:
  1. A candidate must have obtained:
    • At least five (5) passes at the Uganda Certificate of Education or its equivalent at the same sitting;
    • At least one (1) principal pass and two (2) subsidiary passes obtained at the same sitting or its equivalent.
