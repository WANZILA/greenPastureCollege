Green Pasture College Application Guidelines

Green Pasture College is receiving applications for Short courses, Undergraduate diploma, Postgraduate Diploma and Masters programmes for its three intakes, January, May and August.

Below are the application guidelines:

  • Located Mbale city, Industrial Division, Green Pasture village, Musoto with all your academic documents
  • Pick an application form of your choice
  • Fill it up and attach the required application documents
  • Pay the application fee
  • Submit the application form to the registrar of the respective School
